Best Movies According to Ibrahim

My Most Favorite TV series

Vikings

The Vikings series follows the rise of Ragnar Lothbrok as a Viking warrior and his adventures filled with raids. The series focuses on Norse mythology, battles, and family dramas.

Stranger Things

Stranger Things is a sci-fi series set in the 1980s in the town of Hawkins, USA. The series follows a group of kids as they get caught up in secret government experiments and paranormal events. It begins with the disappearance of a boy (Will Byers), and his friends, a girl (Eleven), and his family come together in search of him. The series explores themes like friendship, coming of age, and the existence of supernatural entities. Rich in 80s nostalgia, music, and culture, Stranger Things has received positive reviews from both critics and audiences.

Narcos

Narcos follows the history of Colombia's drug trade, focusing on the story of Pablo Escobar. The series depicts Escobar's rise, the power of the drug cartels, and the U.S. efforts to combat them. It is a dramatic series based on real events.
